About the Game

Baldur's Rush is a competitive racing game within a dark fantasy setting, combining themes of cursed myth with the thrill of fast paced kart competition. Your racing is based on skill however your advancements are dealt with luck.

Choose from a selection of karts that have different capabilities. Avoid obstacles such as alcohol spills that can make you lose control. Use spells to knock your opponents off course, and strategically manipulate your surroundings!
