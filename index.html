About Me

I am an aspiring games designer with over 6 years of experience developing games inside different game engines such as: Unreal Engine, Unity and Godot. I specialise in puzzle design as a main role however I am confident with the generic games design overview. Through this, I’ve worked with students from various Games Design disciplines and backgrounds, gaining a broad understanding of professional development pipelines and collaborative workflows. A collaborative, positive creative environment matters to me just as much as the work itself. I’m genuinely passionate about this industry, and I believe that’s reflected in both my design work and the way I work with others.

My work sits at the intersection of puzzle design, systems thinking, and atmosphere. I believe a great game is an act of world-making: every mechanic should echo the mythology, and every UI element should feel like an artefact from within the world itself.

Currently open to roles in puzzle design, level design, and creative direction.
